Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: January 2026

How We Earn Revenue

StylePulse Picks earns revenue through affiliate marketing. This means that some of the links on our website — particularly product recommendation buttons and shop links — are affiliate links. When you click through these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the affiliate partner.

What This Means for You

Using our affiliate links does not cost you anything extra. The price you pay for any product is the same whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer. We earn a small percentage of the sale as a referral fee — this is how we keep StylePulse Picks running.

Editorial Independence

Our affiliate relationships never influence our editorial content. We recommend products based on quality, value, and relevance to our audience — not on commission rates or affiliate partnerships. If a product is not genuinely worthwhile, we will not recommend it regardless of affiliate potential.

How We Select Products

Our team researches and selects products based on fashion trends, quality, price point, and audience interest. We aim to recommend versatile, well-made items that genuinely help our readers. We prioritize products that align with our editorial focus on timeless style and mindful consumption.

Affiliate Partners

Our primary affiliate partner is MuleBuy (mulebuy.com), a fashion discovery platform that connects global shoppers with Chinese fashion products. We may also work with other affiliate networks and individual brand partnerships.
