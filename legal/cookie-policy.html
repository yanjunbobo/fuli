Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to help websites function efficiently, provide analytics, and deliver personalized content.

How We Use Cookies

StylePulse Picks uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Basic functionality: Some cookies are essential for our site to function properly, such as remembering your preferences or maintaining session information.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors navigate our site, which pages are most popular, and how long visitors spend on our content. This helps us improve our website and content.
  • Advertising: Third-party advertisers may use cookies to deliver relevant advertisements based on your browsing activity. These cookies track your visits across websites to build a profile of your interests.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are placed by third-party services. This includes affiliate partners, advertising networks, and analytics providers. These third parties may collect information about your browsing activity across our site and other websites.

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, block only third-party cookies, or delete existing cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our website.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
